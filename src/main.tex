    \section{Function:}
    The hyperbolic sine function is
    \begin{equation}
        \sinh(x) &= \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{2}
    \end{equation}
    \begin{equation}
        e &= \lim_{x \to \infty}(1+\frac{1}{x})^x
    \end{equation}
    e = 2.718281828459 (approximately)
    \section{Domain and Range:}
    The domain and range of hyperbolic sine function is (-\infty,+\infty).
